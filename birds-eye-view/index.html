Which is the Internet
and which is the Web?

  1. the Internet
    1. a global communication network interconnecting devices
    2. technologies: TPC, IP
  2. the Web
    1. an information space on top of the Internet
    2. technologies: HTTP, HTML, URL

The Internet is to Web what
the telephone network is to fax.

The Web is a layer of interlinked resources accessible through the Internet.

  1. on the Internet but not on the Web
    1. e-mail, Skype, BitTorrent, VPN, …
  2. on the Web but not on the Internet
    1. nothing—by definition

The Web is perhaps the most popular application of the Internet.

  1. Billions of documents from all over the world
    at everyone's fingertips, often free.
    1. access without leaving home
    2. access without leaving your car, train, or airplane
  2. Surfing the Internet or surfing the Web?
    1. The difference is difficult to explain when
      a default Web browser is called “Internet Explorer” ;-)

Most people don't understand or care about the difference… but you should!
